Welcome to Food for Later

Food for Later is an application designed to help users track their food inventory, reduce waste, and plan meals efficiently.

Key Features

  • Manage fridge, freezer, and pantry items.
  • Set expiration dates and receive notifications.
  • Create shopping lists and meal plans.
  • Save and organize recipes.
